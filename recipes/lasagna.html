LASAGNA

Description

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish made by layering flat pasta sheets with fillings such as meat (typically ground beef or sausage), tomato sauce, and béchamel or ricotta cheese. The layers are repeated and topped with cheese, then baked until golden and bubbly. It's a hearty, flavorful, and widely loved comfort food that can also be made vegetarian or with other variations.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef (or sausage)
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato sauce, paste, or crushed tomatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, Italian seasoning
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Egg (for ricotta mixture)

Steps:

  1. Cook the ground meat with onion, garlic, and seasoning.
  2. Add tomato sauce and simmer the mixture.
  3. Boil the lasagna noodles until tender.
  4. Mix ricotta cheese with an egg and a pinch of salt.
  5. Spread a layer of sauce in a baking dish.
  6. Add a layer of noodles over the sauce.
  7. Spread the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
  8. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top.
  9. Repeat the layers until ingredients are used up.
  10. Finish with sauce and cheese on top.
  11. Cover with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25 minutes.
  12. Remove foil and bake for 15 more minutes.
  13. Let it rest for 10–15 minutes before serving.
