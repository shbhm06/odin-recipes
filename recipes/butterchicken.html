BUTTER CHICKEN

Description

Butter chicken is a rich and creamy Indian dish made with marinated chicken cooked in a spiced tomato and butter-based sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup thick yogurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp oil (extra for cooking sauce)
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1.5 cups tomato purée or 3 large tomatoes, puréed
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste (for sauce)
  • 1 tsp red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp sugar (optional)
  • Salt to taste (for sauce)
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 1 tbsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves), crushed
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Steps:

  1. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, salt, and oil. Let it sit for at least 30 minutes (or overnight).
  2. Cook the marinated chicken in a pan or grill until fully done, then set aside.
  3. In another pan, heat butter and oil, then sauté chopped onions until golden.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until the raw smell disappears.
  5. Add tomato purée, red chili powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, salt, and sugar. Cook until the oil separates.
  6. Add cooked chicken pieces and simmer for 5–10 minutes.
  7. Stir in cream and crushed kasuri methi. Simmer for a few more minutes until creamy and well combined.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander and a swirl of cream. Serve hot with naan or rice.
